3 cups beans

1/2 cup crushed stockfish and crayfish

1 cup chopped onions

3 tbs crayfish seasonig

1/4 cup crayfish powder

1 cup of stock from any protein of choice

Smoothly blended smoked ppepper mixture depending on
your pepper intake (scotch bonnet,tomatoes,shombo and onion)

1 cup palm o

800ml water

2 plantains

Sunflower or groundnut oil for frying our plantain


PROCEDURE:

Rinse the beans in a bowl and pour them into a pressure cooker or any protein

Pour the stock in, add half a cup of chopped onions and 800ml water. Ensure the
water covers and lock the pressure cooker before leaving this to cook for 40 mins

Heat up the palm oil in a pot and fry your onions

Add the crushed stockfish and crayfish, crayfish seasoning and crayfish powder

Pour in the blended pepper mixture, stir and leave this to cook for 8 minutes.
(At this stage, you can add a protein of choice if you wish to do so)

Check on the beans and take it off heat

Pour the cooked beans into the pepper mixture ans combine. Once it has been well
incorporated, leave to cook on low heat for about 7-10 mins

Peel your plantain and cut into any desired shape

Heat up your oil and fry the plantain on low heat

Once its all done, serve and enjoy.

3 eggs

1/4 cup milk

Sliced bread

Pinch of black pepper

Pinch of salt

1 stick butter

Baked beans


PROCEDURE:

Break the eggs in a bowl, add salt, a pinch of black pepper and
1/4 cup of milk in a bowl. Beat the eggs for a minute

Heat up a pan and pour the eggs into the pan. Now proceed to add
your stick of butter into the pan. Fry eggs on low heat and scramble
them. Once this is done, take the eggs off heat and serve them in a small bowl

Toast 4 slices of bread in a toaster and pour some baked beans into a small
bowl and serve the dish alongside the scrambled eggs

Serve this with any beverage of choice or freshly squeezed juice

1 cup of palm oil

1 cup onion, chopped

2 tbs grounded crayfish

African spinach/ Efo soko

500g pre boiled chicken

2 tbs crayfish seasoning

Roughly blended pepper mixture(mixture of bell peppers(Tatase)), scotch bonnet
peppers, cayenne pepper(shombo), tomatoes and 1 large onion

300g smoked fish


PROCEDURE:

Heat up a cup of palm oil in pot and stir with chopped onions

Pour the roughly blended pepper mixture into the pot. Ensure the peppers are
not smooth blend but rather roughly blended

Season with crayfish seasoning and crayfish powder

Add the smoked fish and pre boiled chicken in the stew. Stir this and leave
it cook for 15-20 minutes

Cut the spinach from the stem upwards into tiny pieces and rinse it thoroughly
with hot water. Drain the spinach well in a sieve

Finally add the spinach into the stew and allow it to cook for 3-4 mins

Take the vegetables soup off heat and serve with swallow of choice or rice
